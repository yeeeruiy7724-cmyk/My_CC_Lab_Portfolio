Project Description

I used everyday common elements like a coffee cup and a digital clock, with the rise and fall of coffee liquid level symbolizing the passage of time and the cycle of day and night. It's a simple experimental clock artwork, visually conveying to audiences: time flies, as the ancients said, "Time flows like a river", it means every single moment in your life is precious.
